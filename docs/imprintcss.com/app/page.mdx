# Welcome To ImprintCSS

## Introduction

ImprintCSS is a simple responsive CSS toolkit from Stuart Herbert. It has [clearly defined goals](goals). It draws [heavy inspiration](inspirations) from existing toolkits and leading content websites.

It is [licensed under the 3-clause BSD license](license). [Contributions](contributing) are welcome.

## Why Use ImprintCSS?

The benefits of using ImprintCSS include:

* [beautiful typography](typography) for easy reading whether you're on the smallest or the largest of devices
* [convenient components](components) to enhance your content even further and provide nuanced navigation
* [semantic markup](structure) that describes your content and not your layout, based on HTML5 tags
* [utility classes](utilities) when you need to be pragmatic

ImprintCSS is a plugin for Tailwind v3. You can import it into your own `tailwind.config.js`, and quickly build on top of it to get the look and feel that you want for your own websites and content.
